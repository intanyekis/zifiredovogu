    In a solemn announcement that shocked the East Fishkill community, the tragic passing of Officer Daniel DiDato marks a profound loss. DiDato, a respected member of the Fishkill East Police Department, has died from injuries sustained in a vehicle accident while on duty. This incident, which occurred on December 19, 2023, was the first fatality in the history of the town’s police department.

Although he was taken to Mid-Hudson Regional Hospital, DiDato later died from his injuries. News of his passing left the community and his colleagues in a state of deep grief.

    DiDato’s career spanned more than two decades, with a significant portion dedicated to the East Fishkill community. His approach to law enforcement was marked by a deep sense of duty and commitment to public safety, making him a role model to many within the department.

    Following DiDato’s death, the East Fishkill Police Department was in mourning. New York State Police and the Dutchess County Sheriff’s Office stepped in to handle the calls, allowing EFPD officers time to grieve and remember their fallen comrade.
