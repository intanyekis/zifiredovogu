    Did Amelia Gray Hamlin And Harry Hamlin Have Trouble Getting Along In The Past?

And fans of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills will remember one scene that was particularly sad to watch. It highlighted the tough time that the family was having during Amelia's battle with an eating disorder.

    In one episode, Amelia and her sister Delilah Belle had dinner at home with Harry Hamlin and Lisa Rinna. Amelia kept saying that she didn't want to eat and everyone looked really upset.

    Amelia Gray Hamlin posted on Instagram about the episode and said that she was mean to Harry because she was suffering from an eating disorder at the time.
