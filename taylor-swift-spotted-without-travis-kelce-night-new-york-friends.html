    Taylor’s Dress Was Likely A Reference To “Midnights”

    Close

    Taylor was photographed on the town with friends in a stunning outfit, which many fans believe is a reference to her Midnights album. With her long, blonde hair styled down and straight, Taylor rocked a shimmery, black mini dress adorned with sparkling stars and a crescent moon.

    She accessorized with a rhinestone bag, black heels, and a fluffy black jacket, which she wore draped off her shoulders for an effortlessly chic look.

    Even though Travis skipped the soiree, Taylor was joined by a plethora of famous faces, including Blake Lively, Gigi Hadid, Zoë Kravitz, the Haim sisters, and Miles Teller.

    Even though Travis wasn’t there to celebrate last night, which was Taylor’s birthday (December 13th), he’s reportedly got big plans to make it a special weekend for her. Various reports claimed the NFL star was planning to throw a huge party for her – and was willing to spend a pretty penny on it.

    “[Travis] wants to throw the best party possible,” a source claimed to Us Weekly. “Money is not an object.” The outlet added that Travis was planning the soiree for New York and was creating a big guest list filled with her close friends.

    Travis May Still Have Big Plans For Taylor’s Birthday

    A report from a week ago even claimed that Travis could pop the question to Taylor, even though they’ve only been dating since the summer.

    Referring to the fact that Taylor recently moved into Travis’ new $6 million mansion in Kansas, a source told Life & Style things are going smoothly with the couple.

    “They already know they're compatible, but living together for an extended period will be the ultimate test,” the insider dished. “Even Taylor and Travis are surprised at how seamlessly they've been able to blend their lives. It's been effortless.”
