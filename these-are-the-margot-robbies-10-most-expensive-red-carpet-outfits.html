    Margot Robbie pursued her acting career in Australia while studying drama at Somerset College. After appearing in numerous national commercials, soap operas, and movies, Robbie moved to Los Angeles to audition for television shows. While she faced rejections during the initial years, she shifted her attention to films and landed a role in the 2013 romantic comedy About Time. The same year, the actress received her breakthrough role in Martin Scorsese’s The Wolf Of Wall Street, which made her an overnight star. Her critically acclaimed performance earned her a sought-after star in Hollywood, and she has continued to work in movies actively.

    Since her days on the press tour for The Wolf Of Wall Street, Margot Robbie has relied on celebrity stylist Kate Young for her fashionable looks for premieres and award shows. She encompasses chic and sophisticated looks that are hard to forget. Leaning heavily towards Chanel looks, let’s take a glimpse at Margot Robbie’s most expensive red carpet outfits.

    2020 Academy Awards

    Margot Robbie arrived at the 2020 Oscars red carpet as a top contender for Best Supporting Actress for her performance in Bombshell. According to Vogue, she opted for a couture Chanel gown taken from Karl Lagerfeld’s 1994 Spring/Summer look. Her strapless chiffon bustier gown featured a jewel on the bustier made from pearl and pearl tassels. Robbie accessorized her look with Chanel pearl stud earrings rings.

    The 2019 Cannes Film Festival

    Margot Robbie made her Cannes debut in 2019 with the premiere of her star-studded movie Once Upon A Time In Hollywood. She shimmered in a head-to-toe unique Chanel look for the red carpet. Robbie opted for a glittering white mini dress, black sequin trousers, a matching scarf, and heeled boots. The look was taken from the Chanel Spring/Summer 2011 couture collection. She completed her outfit with a simple pair of feathery earrings.

    Birds of Prey London Premiere

    Channeling her anti-hero Harley Quinn look for the Birds Of Prey premiere, Margot Robbie chose a black Dries Van Noten gown from the Spring/Summer 2020 Collection. Her look featured an exposed bra top, a voluminous skirt, and an asymmetrical feather sash. She chose a Regency-era accessory with her pink satin elbow-length gloves.

    2019 Bombshell Premiere

    The actress arrived at the premiere making a style statement with her couture grown. Margot Robbie donned a lilac Giambattista Valli floor-length dress with a dramatic bubble hem, as noted by Harper’s Bazaar. Exaggerated material was added to the dress for a dramatic look, and a floral piece was added to the waistline. She maintained her signature beach waves and a peachy tone for makeup.

    2018 Golden Globe Awards

    Staying true to the #MeToo movement and wearing all black to support the Time’s Up movement, Margot Robbie wore a black Gucci long-sleeved black gown. Her floor-length ensemble had a plunging neckline, stunning bow designs in the front, and silver sequins. She finished her look with Tiffany & Co. jewels and Roger Vivier shoes.

    Once Upon A Time In Hollywood Berlin Premiere

    Jacquemus has slowly become a celebrity-favorite fashion brand, and Margot Robbie showed her love for the designer and chose an olive green gown from the Spring/Summer 2020 collection. The voluminous maxi gown is called The Manosque Dress and features small buttons on the front. Robbie styled the outfit with Tabitha Simmons shoes and Agnes jewelry.

    2019 SAG Awards

    Margot Robbie was a nominee for her performance in Mary Queen of Scots, and the actress did not disappoint as she arrived on the red carpet for the 2019 SAG Awards. As mentioned by Elle, Robbie chose a Chanel gown with a gold semi-sheer bustier and an ivory skirt. There were stripes of gold detailing throughout her skirt, and the look was chosen from Chanel's Metiers d'Art runway in 2018.

    2016 Suicide Squad Premiere

    Among the sea of stars that arrived at the Suicide Squad premiere, Margot Robbie stood out in her Alexander McQueen gown. The column gown was chosen from the Fall 2016 collection and channeled her eccentric character of Harley Quinn. The two-tone dress had a giant gold sequin design, forming a unicorn and a black lace high collar. Robbie finished the look with a simple black clutch.

    I, Tonya UK Premiere

    Margot Robbie wore a sparkling outfit for the London Premiere of her Oscar-nominated movie I, Tonya. She wore a custom Louis Vuitton gown adorned with blue, white, silver and black embellishments. The dress had a modest slit on the left, and Robbie wore simple heels to complete the look. Robbie and Young paid tribute to her on-screen character through the subtle geometric motifs and sequins.

    2018 Academy Awards

    A first-time nominee for I, Tonya, Margot Robbie made heads turn at the 2018 Academy Awards. The actress donned a Chanel gown in white with jewelry also borrowed from the fashion house, as stated by the Hollywood Reporter. Her strapless column gown had crystal detailing along the sleeves, and she completed her look with Roger Vivier heels.

    Margot Robbie has been the house ambassador for Chanel for many years, which opens a wide array of fashion choices from the luxury brand. The actress has also donned outfits from other notable designers to stand out from the crowd. She was last seen making the press tour rounds for her movie Amsterdam and will be seen promoting her film Barbie in 2023.
