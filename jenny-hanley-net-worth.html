    Jenny Hanley has an estimated net worth of $1 million. This is from her career as an actress.

    Hanley’s foray into the world of entertainment includes memorable appearances on both the big and small screens. She graced the James Bond film “On Her Majesty’s Secret Service” in 1969, showcasing her talent alongside the iconic spy. Her filmography also boasts roles in “The Private Life of Sherlock Holmes” (1970) and the Hammer horror film “Scars of Dracula” (1970). On television, she left an imprint with roles in popular series like “Department S,” “The Persuaders!,” and “Return of the Saint.”

    However, it was her role as one of the presenters of the ITV children’s magazine program “Magpie” from 1974 to 1980 that solidified her place in the hearts of viewers. The show became a staple for young audiences, and Hanley’s engaging presence contributed to its success.

    Jenny Hanley seamlessly transitioned into radio, becoming a familiar voice for listeners. Over the years, she has hosted shows on various platforms, including BBC Radio Berkshire, Radio SAGA, and, since February 2021, Boom Radio. Her radio journey reflects her versatility, from playing music to conducting interviews, captivating audiences with her warm and inviting style.
