    09 Jul 2024, 00:00

    One of the stars of Teen Mom 2 walked off the set of Dr. Drew after appearing for a reunion episode of the show.

    In a turbulent and emotional episode of Dr. Drew On Call, the cast of Teen Mom 2 all came on the show to talk about their experiences during and after filming. There were many bombshells dropped during the show, like how Leah Messer was so depressed she was almost suicidal, and how Chelsea Houska doesn’t know how to talk to her daughter about her drug-addicted father.

    Perhaps the biggest bomb dropped was when Dr. Drew had both Jenelle Evans and David Eason on to talk about their relationship following their marriage in September. At first, the questions centered around Jenelle’s mother and the custody battle for her first son Jace, but then moved on to her relationship with Eason.

    “Everyone tries bashing him,” Evans said when the questions seemed to paint her husband in a poor light.

    When Dr. Drew pointed out that Evans seemed to have a preference for “aggressive” men, that was the last straw for Eason. “He’s trying to make her upset and it’s pissing me off,” he said. “I’m leaving.”

    Eason then left the set, with Jenelle following him soon after.

    As Jenelle was leaving she told executive producer Larry Musnik, “I cannot mentally do this anymore, and you can hear that from my therapist.”

    To fill the air, the producers had Jenelle’s ex Nathan Griffin on during the second segment, where he took the opportunity to bash Eason for the way he treats his son, Kaiser. Kaiser is Jenelle’s second son, whose biological father Griffin. The two have joint custody after a lengthy custody battle, with Kaiser spending most of his time with Jenelle and David.

    At one point, Griffin’s mother barged in for a completely unplanned indictment of Eason’s parenting. “I’m terribly frightened for Kaiser,” she said. “I see red flags.”

    She spoke about a time when Kaiser told her that Eason hit him and added reasons to believe that Evans and Eason have a drug problem. She is seeking temporary custody of Kaiser pending further investigation.

    “All I want is someone to protect my grandchild,” she said.
