    Cash Mullins Lubbock, TX Obituary – Accident: In loving memory of Lubbock man killed in tragic motorcycle accident early Sunday.

    The Mullins family, and the large Lubbock community are mourning the tragic and sudden loss of 20-year-old Cash Mullins.

    “A beloved, long-time Lubbock Real Producers Family member has experienced incredible loss. Alicea Mullins, our photographer, has lost her 20-year-old son, Cash, to an accident. Our hearts weep for Alicea and her three children, Keira, Taos, Trinity, and the rest of her family and friends,” Lubbock Real Producers wrote in a social media post.

    According to reports, Cash was in a bicycle accident early Sunday morning, August 27. Unfortunately, he sustained severe injuries from which he died.

    Further details concerning the crash are yet to be made available, as investigation is ongoing.
