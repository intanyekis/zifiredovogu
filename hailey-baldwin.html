    Name:Hailey Bieber

    Hometown:Tuscon, Arizona

    Birthday:November 22, 1996

    About

    Born Hailey Rhode Baldwin, Hailey Bieber is the daughter of actor Stephen Baldwin and graphic designer Kennya Deodato Baldwin.

    Bieber began modeling in 2014 when she was signed to the New York agency Ford Models. In October of that year, she walked her first runway for Topshop. Bieber has also walked the runway for Tommy Hilfiger, Moschino, Tory Burch and Dolce & Gabbana, and she has appeared in campaigns for Ralph Lauren and Guess. She has since retired from runway modeling.

    In 2017, she joined the TBS show Drop the Mic as a cohost alongside Method Man. She has appeared in music videos for Cody Simpson, DJ Khaled and her husband, Justin Bieber. Hailey has often made cameos on the Kardashian-Jenner family’s reality series as a family friend.

    In March 2021, Hailey started her own YouTube channel to share beauty and lifestyle content and give fans a behind-the-scenes look at her projects. She announced the launch of her skincare line, Rhode Skin, in April 2022.

    Hailey began dating Justin in December 2015, and the couple were linked on and off before splitting in 2016. After a brief relationship with Shawn Mendes, she reconciled with Justin in June 2018. The pair got engaged one month later in the Bahamas. They secretly tied the knot at a New York City courthouse in September 2018 before holding a second wedding in South Carolina in September 2019.
