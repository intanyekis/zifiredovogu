    Welcome to the world of movie magic, where the silver screen is awash with the glitz and glamour of some of the greatest talents to grace the camera lens. Today, we're diving into the box office blockbuster world of the one and only Demi Moore, an actress who has left an indelible mark on the industry with her impeccable talent and stunning performances. From drama to comedy and everything in between, Demi Moore has proven time and time again why she's a true force to be reckoned with. So, let's rank the top 10 highest-grossing Demi Moore movies and see just what has made this actress such a staple in Hollywood.

    Passion of Mind - 2000 ($67 Million)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    In this thought-provoking drama, Demi Moore plays a dual role as both a successful businesswoman in the real world and a passionate artist in her dreams. Directed by Alain Berliner, Passion of Mind is a beautifully crafted film that explores the complexities of the human mind and the intersection of our dreams and reality. With Demi delivering a powerful and nuanced performance, the film grossed over $67 million worldwide.

    The Juror - 1996 ($102 Million)

    Via Flickr/Videospace

    In this pulse-pounding crime-thriller, Demi Moore stars as Annie Laird, a single mother selected for jury duty who finds herself threatened by the Mafia. Directed by Brian Gibson, The Juror is a tense and suspenseful film that will keep you on the edge of your seat. With Demi's electrifying performance leading the charge, the film grossed over $102 million worldwide.

    RELATED: The 10 Highest-Grossing Jane Fonda Movies, Ranked

    Striptease - 1996 ($113 Million)

    Via Flickr/Video Collector

    In this unforgettable comedy-drama film, Demi Moore transforms into Erin Grant, a struggling mother who takes on the high-stakes world of stripping to make ends meet. Directed by Andrew Bergman, Striptease was met with mixed reviews but still managed to pack a punch at the box office, grossing over $113 million worldwide. Demi's fearless performance and stunning dance moves continue to captivate audiences to this day.

    G.I. Jane - 1997 ($178 Million)

    Via Flickr/Issuu

    In this action-packed drama film, Demi Moore stars as Lieutenant Jordan O'Neil, a Navy SEAL recruit who faces numerous challenges in a male-dominated training environment. Directed by the visionary Ridley Scott, G.I. Jane is a testament to Demi's versatility and acting prowess, as she brings Lieutenant O'Neil to life with equal parts grit, determination, and sheer athleticism. The film grossed over $178 million worldwide and cemented Demi's reputation as a leading lady in Hollywood.

    Disclosure - 1994 ($214 Million)

    Via Flickr/Auspicious Theater

    In this high-stakes techno-thriller, Demi Moore takes on the role of Meredith Johnson, a powerful executive accused of sexual harassment by her former lover (played by Michael Douglas). Directed by Barry Levinson, Disclosure is a gripping tale of workplace politics and gender dynamics, exploring the complex relationships between men and women in the corporate world. With Demi's commanding performance as Meredith Johnson, the film grossed over $214 million worldwide, solidifying her as a leading lady in Hollywood.

    A Few Good Men - 1992 ($242 Million)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    In this military courtroom drama, Demi Moore shines as Lieutenant Commander JoAnne Galloway, a tough and determined military lawyer. She teams up with Lieutenant Daniel Kaffee (played by Tom Cruise) to defend two US Marines who are accused of murder in what is widely regarded as one of the best military court dramas of all time. Directed by the legendary Rob Reiner, A Few Good Men was a critical and commercial success, grossing over $242 million worldwide and solidifying Demi's status as a Hollywood heavyweight.

    Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle - 2003 ($259 Million)

    Via Flickr/Stan

    In this high-octane action-comedy, Demi Moore joins Cameron Diaz, Drew Barrymore, and Lucy Liu as Madison Lee, a retired Angel who is brought back into action to stop a group of rogue Angels. Directed by McG, Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle is a fun and fast-paced romp that is sure to leave you smiling. With Demi adding some serious star power to the mix, the film grossed over $259 million worldwide.

    RELATED: The 10 Highest-Grossing Ted Danson Movies, Ranked

    Indecent Proposal - 1993 ($266 Million)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    In this steamy romantic drama, Demi Moore takes on the role of Diana Murphy, a wife whose life takes a dramatic turn when she is offered one million dollars by a wealthy businessman (played by Robert Redford) to spend a night with him. Directed by Adrian Lyne, Indecent Proposal was a commercial hit, grossing over $266 million worldwide and leaving audiences everywhere wondering what they would do in Diana's shoes.

    The Hunchback of Notre Dame - 1996 ($325 Million)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    In this animated musical masterpiece, Demi Moore gives voice to the fiery and passionate Esmeralda, a gypsy dancer who captures the heart of the hunchbacked bell-ringer Quasimodo (played by Tom Hulce). Directed by Gary Trousdale and Kirk Wise, The Hunchback of Notre Dame is a timeless classic that brings Victor Hugo's classic novel to life with stunning animation and a captivating soundtrack. The movie grossed over $325 million worldwide and remains a beloved favorite to this day.

    Ghost - 1990 ($505 Million)

    Via Flickr/Issuu

    In this classic romantic fantasy-thriller, Demi Moore wows as Molly Jensen, a woman haunted by the ghost of her murdered husband (played by Patrick Swayze). Directed by Jerry Zucker, Ghost was a critical and commercial smash, grossing over $505 million worldwide and becoming one of the most beloved romantic movies of all time. Demi's heart-wrenching performance as Molly Jensen will stay with audiences for years to come.
