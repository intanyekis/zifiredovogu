    Early Life and Education

    Iacocca, whose full name is Lido Anthony Iacocca, was born in Allentown, Pennsylvania, to Italian-American parents. His parents, Nicola and Antonietta, instilled in him strong values of hard work and determination.

    He attended Lehigh University, where he pursued a degree in industrial engineering. During his time at Lehigh, Iacocca displayed exceptional leadership qualities and was a member of the Phi Gamma Delta fraternity. His education provided him with a solid foundation in engineering principles and problem-solving skills.

    After completing his undergraduate studies, Iacocca continued his education at Princeton University, where he obtained a master’s degree in mechanical engineering. His academic pursuits further expanded his knowledge and opened doors to new opportunities.
