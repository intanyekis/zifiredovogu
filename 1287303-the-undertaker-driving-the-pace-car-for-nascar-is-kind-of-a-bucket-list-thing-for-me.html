    is making the rounds ahead of his looming induction into the WWE Hall of Fame.

    On March 27, “The Phenom” had the opportunity to the pace car for the NASCAR EchoPark Automotive Texas Grand Prix at Circuit of the Americas. Before he did, the legend spoke to the media and shared his excitement about having the chance to be involved with the NASCAR race.

    “I just want to say this first off, it took me less time to get pace car certified than it did to get my actual driver’s license,” said The Undertaker. “But it’s fun. That is a crazy track. I can’t imagine these 40 drivers having to negotiate this thing. It’s gonna be exciting, I can’t wait.”

    “The Deadman” went on to state that driving the car was a dream come true, as he noted that it was on his bucket list. He was also particularly excited to do so in his hometown.

    “I’m excited, the opportunity, it is kind of a bucket list thing for me, and to be able to do it here in my hometown in my home state, that makes it that much more exciting,” said The Undertaker. “So I’m really looking forward to it.”

    Thanks to Stew Myrick from “Sports Guys Talking Wrestling on The Horn Austin” for sharing the audio for this media call.
