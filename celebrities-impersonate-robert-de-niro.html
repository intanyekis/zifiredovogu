    Some of the biggest names in Hollywood are giving us their best Robert De Niro impressions, and the turn out is absolutely hilarious!

    The 2018 Tribeca Film Festival has officially wrapped up, and we’ve got a lot in store for you! Robert De Niro, one of Hollywood’s most famed and legendary actors, has featured in films more times than one can imagine. The actor’s popularity is astonishingly high that it has become a tradition for many people to attempt a De Niro impersonation, and that is exactly what went down at Tribeca 2018. Check out the hilarious clip below!

    Deadline Hollywood sat down with numerous stars that attended the Tribeca Film Festival this year, and upon being interviewed, asked their guests to do their best Robert De Niro impressions. A few celebrities gave it their best shot, while some didn’t even want to try! Actor Ewan McGregor was one of the first to pop up in the video and refused to even give it a shot. “No, we’re not gonna do it,” he jokingly responded.

    RELATED: ACTING DUOS WHO MADE THE MOST MOVIES TOGETHER

    Whereas stars like Paris Hilton doubted their abilities in doing justice to the great De Niro. “I would probably give like the worst De Niro,” Hilton said, while the other guest sitting in on the interview claimed her impression would “probably be a famous meme,” and he’s probably right! Ralph Macchio was up next and willingly gave his best impression of De Niro’s famous line, “little bit, little bit, little bit,” he said.

    After the famous lines came to the notorious De Niro facial expression, we all know the one! Ansel Elgort gave it his best try and hilariously nailed what we all know as that famous “look”. Antonio Banderas probably delivered the best impression out of all the celebrity guests, by tackling the De Niro expression, and mouthing “yeah, yeah, yeah,” as he did it.

    If that wasn’t good enough, many of the Tribeca filmmakers being interviewed then took to the next and most obvious impression of “you talkin’ to me?” Matthew Broderick hilariously summed up the experience that most guests were having, saying it was “this is so embarrassing.” It might be embarrassing for them, but absolutely hilarious for us!
