    Kendra Wilkinson filed for divorce from Hank Baskett then got drunk on social media.

    According to People, the reality TV star used Instagram to share the news she and her husband of almost nine years had decided to end their marriage.

    “Today is the last day of my marriage to this beautiful man. I will forever love Hank and be open but for now we have chosen to go our own ways. “I’m beyond sad and heartbroken because I did believe in forever, that’s why I said yes but unfortunately too much fear has gotten in the way. We are both amazing parents and our kids will be happy n never know the difference other than seeing mama smile,” explained Wilkinson in her message.

    She ended her post by thanking Baskett for their time together, as well as their beautiful children. They are parents to son Hank IV, age eight, and daughter Alijah, age three.

    “Love wins, in this case, it just looks a little funny. Thank you, Hank, for a beautiful 8 years of marriage and 2 beautiful kids. I feel so thankful and blessed,” she concluded.

    Later that same evening the Kendra On Top star kicked back with a drink and some video games as she took to Instagram stories to entertain her followers. She used the bunny ears filter on herself and was holding what appeared to be a shot glass. Wilkinson also had a headset and a video game controller in her hands.

    “Lol drunk bunny gamer” captioned the former Playboy Playmate. She also used the hashtag #fortnite, which is a reference to the free-to-play game taking over the world.

    It was the end to a hard but busy day for Wilkinson. Along with confirming she and Baskett were getting a divorce, she also played golf with actor John Lovitz and put together a puzzle with her daughter. Both activities were documented on Instagram stories.
